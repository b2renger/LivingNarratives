# State : Scene3

## Description :
Elara finds herself trapped in a dense, magical forest. The trees seem to watch her.

## Possible outputs :

- Click TO Scene2 : The grove is terrifying. Elara focuses all her energy on simply finding her way out, eventually emerging exhausted on the outskirts of the City of Merchants.
- Click TO Scene4 : While navigating the grove, Elara stumbles upon ancient ruins that lead into a dark, forgotten tunnel. She decides to follow it, discovering it leads directly under the King’s Vault.
- Click TO Scene5 : Elara is captured by a massive forest troll. She narrowly escapes, but is forced to flee all the way back to her village, losing all her progress and gear.
